# Lantern Strategic

## Helping businesses move forward when the path isn't obvious.

Every business reaches points where the next move matters more than the last hundred: the right call now sets up years of better operating, and the wrong one quietly costs you ground. The difference usually isn't more information, it's having someone experienced in the room while you think it through.

Lantern Strategic is an operations and technology consulting firm. We bring experienced operational thinking and practical technology to the unnoticed friction slowing your business down.

---
## The problems that don't announce themselves.

Most of the friction in a business doesn't announce itself. It shows up as the third meeting about the same thing, a process that everyone has a workaround for, the monthly report that takes a full day to pull together which was originally built to answer a question nobody asks anymore, or the recurring meetings that have outlived their purpose.
  
These quiet problems are the ones easiest to live with and hardest to fix from the inside. The friction becomes the background. The workaround becomes the process. And the cost compounds in ways nobody's tracking. Lantern Strategic is built to address the quiet problems.

---
## Three ways to engage.

**Defined projects.** Clear scope, flat fee, a specific outcome. Technology evaluations, AI training, focused operational assessments. When you already know what you need handled.

**Discovery and plan.** We sit with the business for a defined period, learn how it actually runs, and produce a plan you can execute or hand back to us to run. When you know something needs to change but naming it cleanly is part of the work.

**Embedded expertise.** An engagement that puts senior operations and technology thinking on your team — fractional executive support, leadership coaching, on-site training, office hours, a sounding board for the calls that don't fit anywhere else. When the value is in having someone to think with, not just a deliverable to receive.

Engagements are scoped to fit. Pricing is project-based or retainer.
